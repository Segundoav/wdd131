Website Planning: Inmuebles Perú

Suggested domain: House sales

Site Name

House sales: This name reflects a website for selling and renting properties across the country with a modern, professional, and trustworthy approach.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase properties for sale or rent in different regions of Peru, providing descriptions, images, contact details, and a contact form for real estate agents.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find houses in Lima with more than 3 bedrooms?
  • How do I contact an agent to visit an apartment in Cusco?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Blue (#1E3ABA): for headers and the header background.
  • White (#FFFFFF): for main text over dark backgrounds.
  • Light Gray (#F3F4F6): for section backgrounds.

Typography

  • Montserrat: for headings (h1, h2, etc.).
  • Roboto: for body text.
